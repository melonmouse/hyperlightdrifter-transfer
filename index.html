Hyper Light Drifter Save Transfer Helper


Transfering a save from one computer to another in Hyper Light Drifter is not as easy as it should be! This page aims to fix that.

You will need two save files for this to work. One from the computer you're transferring from and one from the computer you're transferring to. If you don't have a save on the computer you're transfering to just start a new game and exit once you're past the initial cutscenes.

These save files can be found in the following locations depending on your OS...

  • PC - C:\Users\[username]\AppData\Local\HyperLightDrifter
  • Mac - /Users/[username]/Library/Application Support/com.HeartMachine.HyperLightDrifter (while in Finder open the "Go" toolbar and hold "option" to reveal a quick way to get to the Library directory)

Open these files in Notepad (PC) or TextEdit (Mac) and copy their contents into the fields below. Don't worry that it looks like gibberish! It's all good :)
